WHO ARE WE

Get to know about our company

Checkpoint Dispatching and Logistics LLC was born from a passion to deliver high-quality load dispatching service directly to small/medium fleet and owner operators. We are family owned with he mission to negotiate, trip plan, and ultimately maximize profits for our business partners.

By leveraging over 8 years of experience and knowledge in the transportation industry, Checkpoint dispatching and Logistics LLC works diligently to lock in high paying loads for our partners. We specialize mostly in Dry Vans, Reefers, and Flatbeds. We accept power only and box trucks on a referral or as needed basis.
